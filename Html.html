Roxy Vermilion

Roxy Vermilion (ロキシー・ヴァーミリオン) He is a person who was part of the Long Dream Incident after the destruction of the old world (Earth), Strong with (Copying Ability) Ability Level (Level S+) Weak with (No Ability) Level (Level B), He is the Leader of Squad Moriganne (An organization that has goal to get out of the dream world and the group that joins has the same goal).

He is a person who is obsessed with the power of his hallucinations that come true (Chuunibyou), but when he enters the dream world of another world he gains the power (Copying Abilility) which is able to easily copy someone's weakest (Level E) or strongest (Level S+) abilities. , he also has intermediate level martial arts, intermediate level thinking ability and the ability to adapt to high level situations by adjusting the situation he can resemble anyone he will see (After Using the Ability) For now Characters that have been used are Akane Mizuki, Ninja, Clown the Circus, Kiel (In Light Novel)
